# EMAPOFPI Roadmap

Official development roadmap for the EMAPOFPI platform - Pi Network's seller onboarding platform for Egypt, MENA, and Africa.

## Vision

Build the most trusted, civic-first marketplace for Pi Network, empowering sellers and buyers across Egypt, MENA, and Africa with transparent, secure, and accessible trading.
